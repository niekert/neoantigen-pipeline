DEK-AFF2 Fusion — Immunogenicity Assessment Report
============================================================

WHAT THIS REPORT IS
----------------------------------------
This report goes beyond HLA binding to assess whether the
fusion peptides are likely to be recognized as FOREIGN by
the immune system. The key metric is agretopicity (DAI).

AGRETOPICITY (DAI) — WHAT IT MEANS
----------------------------------------
DAI = IC50_wildtype / IC50_fusion

- DAI > 1: Fusion peptide binds HLA BETTER than the normal
  version. This is good — the immune system's T cells that
  recognize the normal peptide were deleted (tolerance), so
  a stronger-binding fusion peptide is more likely to be
  recognized as foreign.
- DAI > 5: Strong differential. Very promising.
- DAI > 10: Excellent. The fusion peptide binds 10x better.
- DAI < 1: The normal peptide actually binds better. This
  means T cells might already be tolerant to it. Less ideal.

For fusion neoantigens (vs point mutations), high DAI is
expected because the junction sequence is completely novel —
the wildtype counterpart is a different protein entirely.
