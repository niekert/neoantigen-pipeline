WHAT THIS REPORT IS
----------------------------------------
This report extends the HLA binding output with an
exploratory agretopicity (DAI) comparison against WT
DEK- and AFF2-side peptides.

AGRETOPICITY (DAI) — WHAT IT MEANS
----------------------------------------
DAI = IC50_wildtype / IC50_fusion

- DAI > 1: The fusion peptide binds HLA better than the
  chosen wildtype comparator for this allele. That can be
  useful for prioritization, but it does not prove
  presentation or T-cell recognition.
- DAI > 5: Larger differential vs the chosen comparator.
- DAI > 10: Very large differential vs the chosen comparator.
- DAI < 1: The chosen wildtype comparator binds similarly or
  better, which makes the fusion peptide less distinctive by
  this heuristic.

For fusion neoantigens, high DAI can occur because the
junction sequence differs substantially from each native side.
That still does not model processing, abundance, or TCR
recognition.

This report shows BOTH DEK-side and AFF2-side WT comparisons.
For ranking, it uses the LOWER available DAI as a conservative
summary rather than keeping only the most favorable value.
