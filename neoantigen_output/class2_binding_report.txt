WHY CLASS II MATTERS
----------------------------------------
Class II HLA molecules (HLA-DR, -DQ, -DP) present peptides to
CD4+ helper T cells. These helper cells are critical for mounting
and sustaining a strong CD8+ killer T cell response. A vaccine
that activates both Class I (killer) and Class II (helper)
responses is more likely to be effective.

HOW TO READ THESE RESULTS
----------------------------------------
- IC50 (nM): Lower = stronger binding.
- Rank (%): Percentile rank. Lower = better.
  < 2% = strong binder, < 10% = weak binder (Class II thresholds)
- Core peptide: The 9-residue binding core within the 15mer.
