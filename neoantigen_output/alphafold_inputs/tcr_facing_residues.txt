DEK-AFF2 Fusion — TCR-Facing Residue Analysis
============================================================

WHAT THIS MEANS
----------------------------------------
HLA class I molecules bind peptides in a groove. The peptide
is held by anchor residues buried at each end. The middle
residues stick up like a ridge — that's what the T-cell
receptor (TCR) scans for. If those residues look foreign,
the T cell fires.

Anchor positions are allele-specific (different HLA alleles
prefer different amino acids at P2 and P9). TCR-facing
positions determine immunogenicity.
